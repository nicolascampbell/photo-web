Nicolas de Souza Gomez is a 21-year-old gym assistant who enjoys badminton, social card games and golf. He is brave and generous, but can also be very evil and a bit rude.

He is Uruguayan who defines himself as straight. He finished school and then left academia. He is allergic to milk.

Physically, Nicolas is in pretty good shape. He is tall with bronze skin, blonde hair and blue eyes.

He grew up in a middle class neighbourhood. He was raised by his mother, his father having left when he was young.
